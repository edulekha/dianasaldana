The Story

Beauty, Fashion &
a Life Well-Lived

Hi, I'm Diana Saldana — a New York/New Jersey-based beauty creator sharing everything I love about makeup, skincare, fashion, and the beautiful moments of everyday life.

Before stepping into the world of content creation, I walked the stage as Miss Dominican Republic USA and Miss Manhattan New York USA. That journey taught me that beauty isn't about perfection — it's about confidence, authenticity, and celebrating who you are.

Today, I'm proud to be recognized as a Top 1% US Beauty Creator on Instagram, with a community of over 1 million across platforms. My content focuses on making luxury beauty accessible — honest reviews, detailed tutorials, and real recommendations you can trust.
